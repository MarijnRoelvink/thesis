Goal:

  Develop a system that can monitor the validity of projected Food Insecurity scenarios based on predicting whether the underlying assumptions for these scenarios still hold.

4. Implement

This is about:
  • Understanding the problem
  • Finding out what the needs are of the intended users.
How:
  • Interviews with stakeholders
  • Literature study
Research questions:
RQ 1: How are the assumptions underlying the classifications developed?
  • How do experts decide on which data they should focus?
  • Which type of arguments/justifications are used for classifications? Which of those can be formalized?
    • Which arguments are more universally reusable based on knowledge and evidence? Which arguments are more based on personal estimations?
  • How are risk factors determined on a national level and a regional level?
    • How do these two levels interact?
Deliverables:
  • A set of user-requirements for the monitoring tool
  • A literature study on:
    • The state-of-the-art within this field
    • The possible models to use for forecasting and nowcasting FI
    • The main theories and drivers behind FI
